About Us

A few words about us

Welcome to Farm Frescio - an initiative to help farmers through every aspect of their Farming Journey

Farm Frescio website is a one stop solution for facilitating farmers to find a reliable buyer and sell their organic produce and promoting organic farming and its benefits. This portal caters various stakeholders like local groups, individual farmers, buyers and input suppliers. It also provides various models and features to help farmers solve various problems that they might face during their farming journey. Farm Frescio is an E-commerce as well as a knowledge platform. Knowledge repository section of the portal includes latest news, various models including pest prediction, disease prediction and a multilingual agricultural bot to answer any and every question that might pop up during the hectic expedition that a farming process involves to facilitate and promote organic farming. E-commerce section of the portal provides the whole bouquet of products ranging from grains, pulses, fruits and vegetables for which Mandi API can be referred and farmers can decide the best price for their produce. It is also an excellent platform for buyers who can directly view a farmer's location and contact number and get best produce directly from the farmer thereby eliminating the middlemen.

Why we are different

Farm Frescio is an initiative designed specifically to help farmers through evry phase of their farming journey. It includes various features namely an e-commerce platform to facilitate easier buying and selling of crops at reasonable prices by eliminating the middle man, an agricultural bot to answer every question related to farming and other aspects and a fertiliser recommendation system to help farmers control the NPK values of their soil. The website also incorporates a pest predicton and disease prediction system to assist farmers in identifying pests and any diseases that might be affecting their crop. Hence, the website is a one stop solution for all farmers and aims to uplift farmers of Punjab.
