Welcome to Farm Frescio

A one stop solution to help farmers through every aspect of their Farming Journey! Farm Frescio website is an initiative for facilitating farmers to find a reliable buyer and sell their organic produce and promoting organic farming and its benefits. This portal caters various stakeholders like local groups, individual farmers, buyers and input suppliers. It also provides various models and features to help farmers solve various problems that they might face during their farming journey.

Our Services

Farm Frescio includes various features namely an e-commerce platform to facilitate easier buying and selling of crops at reasonable prices by eliminating the middle man, an agricultural bot to answer every question related to farming and other aspects and a fertiliser recommendation system to help farmers control the NPK values of their soil. The website also incorporates a pest predicton and disease prediction system to assist farmers in identifying pests and any diseases that might be affecting their crop. Hence, the website is a one stop solution for all farmers and aims to uplift farmers of Punjab.
